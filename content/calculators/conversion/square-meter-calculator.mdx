## How This Calculator Works

This tool operates in two modes. **Dimensions mode** multiplies length by width to compute area directly in square meters, then converts to all other area units. **Convert mode** takes an existing area value in any supported unit and converts it to square meters and all other units simultaneously.

The critical conversion factor between the two most commonly searched units is: **1 square meter = 10.7639 square feet**. This factor is not intuitive because it is the square of the linear conversion (1 meter = 3.28084 feet, and 3.28084 squared = 10.7639). Many people mistakenly divide by 3.28 instead of 10.76 when converting area, producing a result that is off by a factor of 3.3.

For calculating area from room dimensions in feet with shape options (rectangle, circle, triangle, trapezoid), use our [Square Footage Calculator](/calculators/construction/square-footage-calculator). For converting between all 10 area unit types including square kilometers and square miles, see the [Area Converter](/calculators/conversion/area-converter).

## The Formula

**Dimensions mode:**

$$\text{Area (m}^2\text{)} = \text{Length (m)} \times \text{Width (m)}$$

If inputs are in feet, convert first: length (m) = length (ft) x 0.3048

**Convert mode:**

$$\text{Square Meters} = \text{Area Value} \times \text{Conversion Factor to m}^2$$

**Conversion factors to square meters:**

| From | Multiply by |
|:--|:--|
| Square Feet (ft²) | 0.09290304 |
| Square Yards (yd²) | 0.83612736 |
| Acres | 4,046.8564224 |
| Hectares | 10,000 |

**From square meters to other units:**

$$\text{sq ft} = \text{m}^2 \times 10.7639 \qquad \text{sq yd} = \text{sq ft} \div 9 \qquad \text{sq in} = \text{sq ft} \times 144$$

$$\text{acres} = \text{m}^2 \div 4{,}046.86 \qquad \text{hectares} = \text{m}^2 \div 10{,}000$$

*Source: NIST Special Publication 330, "The International System of Units (SI)" (2019 Edition). Linear factor: 1 foot = 0.3048 m exactly per the International Yard and Pound Agreement (1959). Area factors derived by squaring linear factors.*

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I convert square feet to square meters?

Divide the square footage by 10.7639 to get square meters. For example, 200 sq ft / 10.7639 = 18.58 square meters. The conversion factor 10.7639 is the square of the linear factor 3.28084 (feet per meter). A common mistake is dividing by 3.28 instead of 10.76, which produces a result that is 3.28 times too large.

### How many square feet is 1 square meter?

One square meter equals exactly 10.7639 square feet. Visualize it as a square roughly 3 feet 3 inches on each side. For quick mental math, multiply square meters by 10.76 or, for a rougher estimate, multiply by 11 and subtract 2%.

### How do I calculate square meters from feet measurements?

If your room is measured in feet, first convert each dimension to meters by multiplying by 0.3048, then multiply length by width. For a 12 x 10-foot room: (12 x 0.3048) x (10 x 0.3048) = 3.6576 x 3.048 = 11.15 square meters. Alternatively, calculate square footage first (120 sq ft), then divide by 10.7639 to get 11.15 m².

### What is the difference between this calculator and the area converter?

This calculator focuses on computing area in square meters from room dimensions (length x width) and converting between the five most common area units. The [Area Converter](/calculators/conversion/area-converter) is a general-purpose converter that supports 10 area units including square millimeters, square centimeters, square kilometers, and square miles, but does not calculate area from dimensions.
